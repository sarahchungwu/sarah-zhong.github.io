Research Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.

The principles of neuroplasticity include "use it or lose it", repetition, intensity, timing, plasticity is a two-way street, novelty, and the whole brain is involved. By understanding these principles, we can use neuroplasticity to improve our cognitive and emotional abilities and overcome challenges.


Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit.

To engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for my benefit as a student of coding, I could set small goals, establish a regular study routine, use repetition to reinforce learning, challenge myself, prioritize self-care, and seek out feedback and support. By doing so, I can improve my coding skills, manage my time better, and support my brain health.


What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

To increase my neuroplasticity, I will try exercising regularly, learning new things, practicing mindfulness, getting enough sleep, reducing stress, doing brain exercises, and eating a healthy diet. These practices can help improve my brain's ability to change and adapt.

Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

Mindset refers to a person's attitudes and beliefs about their abilities and potential. It plays a critical role in determining how individuals approach challenges, setbacks, and opportunities for growth.As a student, mindset is relevant because it can impact how I approach my studies, my willingness to take on challenging assignments, and my ability to persist in the face of setbacks.


In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

This mindset exploration surprised me by highlighting the power of praise. I used to underestimate its importance, but now I realize that effective praise can motivate and foster a growth mindset. I've decided to start giving specific, meaningful praise focused on the process of learning and growth to myself and others. This exploration has inspired me to become a more thoughtful and effective praiser.

How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

To integrate a growth mindset into my learning journey, I will embrace challenges, learn from failures, believe in effort and practice, set realistic goals, seek feedback and support, and stay motivated. These principles will help me develop a growth mindset that can help me overcome challenges and continue to develop my coding skills.
