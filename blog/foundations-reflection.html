By adopting a growth mindset, I am more open to challenges, more resilient in the face of setbacks, and more motivated to strive for success. This mindset has already helped me to approach my learning with more enthusiasm and determination, and I believe it will continue to serve me well in my future endeavours.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

In my opinion, the programming school is focusing on core learning in the web development Bootcamp course because it recognizes the importance of developing well-rounded and effective programmers.
